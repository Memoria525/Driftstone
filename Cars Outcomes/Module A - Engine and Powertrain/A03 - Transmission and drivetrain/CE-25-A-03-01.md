## Compare manual and automatic transmissions

# Manual and Automatic Transmissions

A lecture for automotive technology students

Learning objective: Compare how manual and automatic transmissions change gear ratios.

---

## Why Gears Exist

An engine makes useful power only over a limited range of speeds. The transmission matches engine speed to road speed by selecting gear ratios.

- A **manual** transmission uses a driver-operated clutch and gear lever to select ratios by hand.
- An **automatic** transmission selects ratios itself, traditionally using a fluid torque converter and planetary gearsets.

---

## Trade-offs

- Manuals give direct driver control and are mechanically simple.
- Automatics reduce driver workload and can shift more smoothly in traffic.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Transmissions exist to keep the engine in its useful speed range as road speed changes.
- Manuals are driver-shifted; automatics shift themselves.
